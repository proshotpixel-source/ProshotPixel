Important Notes

Please download your raw and edited data within 8 days. We will not be responsible after data closure.
Advance booking payment is needed, and the remaining payment should be cleared on the shoot day.
Please carry clean shoes in your bag for use inside the studio only.
Please carry suitable jewellery, as we provide only makeup and hairstyling.
Clients are requested to be on time; a delay of 15–20 minutes is acceptable, but any further delay will result in reduced shoot time.
Soft drink and food is not allowed in studio & during shoot.
Please take care of your child during the shoot, and kindly do not mess with the beautiful sets.

Extra members are not allowed in the studio.
Clients are requested to take care of their belongings during a shoot. No staff/photographer would be responsible for any loss.
Edited photos do not include background changes as we already provide premium sets; only one photo can be edited with a background change or AI editing.
